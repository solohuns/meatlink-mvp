About MeatLink

MeatLink is a wholesale marketplace designed to give farmers a direct channel to buyers. We focus on bringing Eastern Cape farmers closer to Western Cape township markets.

How It Works

  1. Farmers register and list available meat stock.
  2. Resellers place bulk orders directly from farmers.
  3. Cold-storage transport ensures meat quality.
  4. Payments are seamless and secure for all parties.

Why Join?

  • 🚜 Farmers keep more profit by avoiding middlemen.
  • 🏪 Township buyers get affordable, fresh stock.
  • 🚚 Cold-chain trucks guarantee freshness.
  • 💳 Secure transactions with escrow-style payments.
